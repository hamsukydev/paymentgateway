Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and payment services.

What payment methods can I accept with HamsukyPay?

HamsukyPay allows you to accept payments via credit/debit cards, bank transfers, USSD, QR codes, and mobile money options like MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and others. The specific payment methods available depend on your subscription plan.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. The fees listed are the only fees you'll pay. There are no setup fees, monthly maintenance fees, or hidden charges. You only pay the subscription fee and the per-transaction fee for successful transactions.

How do refunds work?

You can issue full or partial refunds to your customers through our dashboard or API. For full refunds, the transaction fee is also refunded. For partial refunds, the transaction fee is not refunded. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days depending on the customer's bank.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your merchant dashboard. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

How long does it take to receive my payments?

For standard plans, settlements are made within 24-48 hours (excluding weekends and holidays). Business and Professional plans have the option for T+1 settlements (next business day). Enterprise customers can negotiate custom settlement terms including same-day settlements.
