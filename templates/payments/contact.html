Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about HamsukyPay

How long does it take to set up an account?

Setting up a HamsukyPay account takes only a few minutes. After registering, you'll need to complete verification which typically takes 1-2 business days depending on the information provided.

What payment methods do you support?

We support a wide range of payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, mobile money, USSD, and QR code payments. Our platform is constantly expanding to incorporate new payment options across Africa and beyond.

How quickly are funds settled to my account?

Standard settlement is within 24-48 hours, but we also offer options for instant settlement for verified merchants. Enterprise customers can negotiate custom settlement schedules based on their business needs.

What are your transaction fees?

Our transaction fees start at 1.5% for standard plans, with volume discounts available. We offer competitive rates compared to other payment providers in the market. Visit our pricing page for detailed information about our fee structure.
